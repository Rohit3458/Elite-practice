A paragraph is a self-contained unit of writing that expresses a particular point or idea. It typically begins with a topic sentence that introduces the main idea, followed by supporting sentences that elaborate on that idea. Paragraphs are essential for organizing thoughts in essays, research papers, and other forms of writing, helping to create a clear and coherent structure.
